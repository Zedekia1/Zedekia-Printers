Company Background

 Zedekia Printers (Private) Limited is one of Zimbabwe’s leading companies with an exceptional reputation of world-class quality, dedicated and excellent service delivery. Since establishment in 2008, the company has since exploded as the preferred supplier of Printing, Packaging, Corporate branding, Advertising, Stationery and related services around Zimbabwe and the region.

The company’s outstanding reputation is based on the founders’ running core principles which include the delivery of top-quality products, a commitment to excellent service delivery not forgetting the passion driven determination for printing.

We love everything that we do. We passionately encourage open and honest relationships with customers, suppliers and our efficient staff whenever it comes to support. We always aim to build long-term partnerships that clients can always trust through very competitive prices, hence supporting your business throughour fair prices.

Zedekia Printers (Private) Limited operates in an environment fully equipped with an impressive array of state of the-art pre-press, original Heidelberg printing, packaging and finishing equipment capable of high speeds production prestigious high quality printing up to A1 size. The entire plant runs 7 days a week offering unsurpassed quality products and turn-around time.

Working with us an absolute pleasure and our production of your printing work is a splendid experience all of which is fully driven by our highly skilled and experienced team of specialists aimed and focused on a single objective of perfection.

Our Head office is also equipped with a design studio with vibrant, brilliant, enthusiastic and highly creative graphic designers who are committed to satisfy our customers’ creative and branding needs.

We, therefore, remain a forever expanding and innovative company with a clear vision of future direction in graphics and printing needs all achieved through the observations and implementations of innovative market trends.
